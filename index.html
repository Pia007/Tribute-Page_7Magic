Seven Magic Mountains

Created by Ugo Rondinone

Here is a timeline of the creation of the Seven Magic Mountains

  • December 2015: Ugo 's vision begin's to materialize as selected boulders are cut.
  • December 2015: The boulders are cored in preparation of stacking.
  • December 2015: The boulders are carefully stacked.
  • April 2016: The boulders are painted.
  • May 2016: Seven Magic Mountains opens to the public.
